RainyDay is a web application that displays the current weather based on your location. We provide the current, previous, hourly, and 7-day weather report from anywhere in the US. Users who subscribe to our site can customize their home page based on their preference.

We provide the latest weather report using an external API. You can find more information about it here.

Enter your city or state in the provided field to retrieve the latest weather report.
